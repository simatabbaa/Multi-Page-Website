British Shorthair

The British Shorthair is the pedigreed version of the traditional British domestic cat, with a distinctively stocky body, thick coat, and broad face. British shorthairs are good pets and loyal companions.

Ragdoll

The Ragdoll is a breed of cat with a distinct colorpoint coat and blue eyes. The Ragdoll is a placid cat but does not really go limp when you hold her.
